# Export and publish a Mind Map app

This guide covers how to save, share, and publish a completed Mind Map Studio knowledge graph so that other users can access it.

## Prerequisites

- A completed Mind Map app (see [Author a Mind Map app](authoring-workflow.md))
- DIAL Chat access with sharing or publication permissions

## Save the app

1. In Mind Map Studio, click **Save and exit**. The app is saved to your My Workspace and is accessible only to you.

The saved app appears in My Workspace alongside your other applications. You can reopen it in Mind Map Studio at any time to edit sources, content, or styling.

## Share with specific users

Sharing gives selected users access to your Mind Map app without making it visible to the entire organization.

2. In My Workspace, locate your Mind Map app and open the sharing dialog.
3. Generate a sharing link. You can optionally grant editing or re-sharing permissions.
4. Send the link to the intended recipients.

Recipients see the shared app in their My Workspace. You retain ownership and can revoke access at any time.

## Publish to the Marketplace

Publication makes your Mind Map app available to all authenticated users (or a restricted audience) through the Marketplace. Publication requires administrator approval.

5. Submit a publication request for your Mind Map app.
6. An administrator reviews and approves or rejects the request through DIAL Admin.
7. Once approved, the app appears in the Marketplace and is discoverable by other users.

**Note**
> Published apps are read-only for consumers. If you need to update the graph, edit the app in Mind Map Studio and resubmit for publication.


## Update a published app

To update a Mind Map app after publication:

8. Open the app in Mind Map Studio from My Workspace.
9. Make your changes — update sources, regenerate the graph, or adjust styling.
10. Click **Save and exit** to save the updated version.
11. Resubmit the app for publication to push the changes to the Marketplace.

**Tip**
> If your source material has changed, you can update the sources in the Sources section and regenerate the graph without rebuilding from scratch.
