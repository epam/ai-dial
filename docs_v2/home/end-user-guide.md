# End user guide

In this short tutorial, you will start using DIAL Chat: sign in, hold your first conversation with a model, and find applications in the Marketplace. By the end, you will know how to do the everyday tasks DIAL Chat is built for. This path is for people who use DIAL Chat to get work done, not to build or operate it. No technical background is required.

## Prerequisites

- Access to a DIAL Chat instance. This may be your organization's deployment or the hosted [DIAL SaaS edition](https://chat.dialx.ai/).
- Sign-in credentials for that instance.

## Step 1: Sign in

Open your DIAL Chat URL and sign in with your account.

**Verify:** the chat interface loads with an empty conversation and a model selector.

## Step 2: Have your first conversation

1. Select a language model from the model selector.
2. Type a question or request in the message box and send it.

**Verify:** the model responds in the conversation, and the exchange is saved in your conversation history.

## Step 3: Find applications in the Marketplace

The [Marketplace](../chat-user-guide/3.marketplace-and-apps.md) is where you discover applications and agents — ready-made tools for tasks such as document analysis, summarization, and knowledge exploration.

1. Open the Marketplace from the sidebar.
2. Browse or search for an application, then add it to your workspace.

**Verify:** the application appears in your workspace and is ready to use.

## What you learned

- How to sign in to DIAL Chat and choose a model
- How to hold and save a conversation
- How to find and add applications from the Marketplace
