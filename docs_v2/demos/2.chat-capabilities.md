# Chat capabilities

DIAL Chat is the default end-user interface of the platform. These demos show what daily use looks like: talking to models and apps, discovering them in the marketplace, and collaborating with colleagues.

## DIAL Chat

A tour of the chat experience — a customizable interface with enterprise-grade access control and the ability to add custom GenAI applications alongside models.

https://youtu.be/W5fpFW7XDn4

## Marketplace

The Marketplace is one entry point for every application, agent, tool, and model, with tagging, search, and filtering — all under the same permissions and roles as the rest of the platform.

https://youtu.be/c7gnOTTupB8

Marketplace 2.0 adds the Application Builder for real-time agent editing, a Table View, richer metadata, and direct links for sharing.

https://youtu.be/OhOf_g1QcLI

## Collaboration

Three ways to collaborate inside your organization — Share, Export, and Publish — and when to reach for each.

https://youtu.be/ywmzhAuFMaI

## Publications

Publish conversations, prompts, files, and applications to specific people or teams through a review-and-approval workflow, with version history so updates do not overwrite the original.

https://youtu.be/GxdhfWLspa0

## Parameterized Replay

Turn a conversation into a reusable, shareable template: others follow the same flow but supply their own inputs for a personalized result — useful for repeatable business workflows.

https://youtu.be/OUkxS8wu9k0

## Animated Scatterplot

A quick look at chat producing a live, animated Plotly visualization from data — generated on the fly through AI code generation.

https://youtu.be/t8MBktPJZDY
