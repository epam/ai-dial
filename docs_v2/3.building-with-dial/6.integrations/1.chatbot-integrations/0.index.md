# Chatbot integrations

Chatbot integrations bring DIAL's models and applications into the messaging and conversation platforms your users already work in. Instead of switching to a separate window, users interact with DIAL through a bot or connector while DIAL enforces its existing access control. This section is for app developers integrating DIAL with conversational platforms.
